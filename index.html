🌿 About Mbwambo Boutique
🏪 Our Store — Kariakoo, DSM

Mbwambo Boutique is a unique fashion store in Kariakoo, Dar es Salaam. Founded in 2019 by Ombeni Mbwambo, we blend African tradition with modern style.

We sell kitenge, leso, Ankara blazers, shoes, bags and premium accessories made by skilled Tanzanian artisans.
